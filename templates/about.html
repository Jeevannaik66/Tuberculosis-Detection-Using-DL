Our Mission & Vision

Dedicated to pioneering innovative technologies for accurate tuberculosis diagnosis and prevention

Our Mission

To revolutionize tuberculosis detection through advanced AI technology, making accurate diagnosis accessible to everyone worldwide. We strive to reduce TB mortality rates by enabling early detection and timely intervention.

Our Vision

A world free from tuberculosis, where advanced technology and compassionate care work together to eliminate this preventable disease. We envision a future where no one suffers from undiagnosed or late-diagnosed TB.

Our Commitment

We are committed to continuous innovation, ethical AI development, and global health equity. Our team works tirelessly to improve detection accuracy and make our technology available to underserved communities.

How accurate is your AI in detecting tuberculosis?
Our deep learning model achieves over 95% accuracy in detecting tuberculosis patterns from chest X-rays. It's trained on thousands of validated medical images and continuously improves through machine learning and clinical feedback.
Is TB Detect available for healthcare organizations?
Yes, we partner with hospitals, clinics, and healthcare organizations worldwide. Our technology integrates with existing systems and can be customized to meet specific workflow requirements.
How do you ensure patient data privacy and security?
We adhere to strict data protection regulations including HIPAA and GDPR. All medical images are anonymized, encrypted, and processed securely. We never share patient data without explicit consent.
Can your technology work in low-resource settings?
Absolutely. Our platform is designed to work with basic internet connectivity and can process images from standard X-ray machines. We're committed to making TB detection accessible in all healthcare environments.
How can I get involved with TB Detect?
We welcome partnerships with researchers, healthcare providers, and organizations working in TB prevention. Contact us through our website to discuss collaboration opportunities or career openings.
